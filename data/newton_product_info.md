# Newton AI – Product Information

**Product Name:** Newton AI

## 🧠 What is Newton AI?

Newton AI is a powerful platform built by Ravian AI that accelerates enterprise AI adoption by removing technical complexity and lowering the barrier to entry. It empowers **data analysts**—not just data scientists—to build and deploy machine learning models using Python, without needing deep expertise in data science or engineering.

---

## ⚙️ Key Features

- **No-Code + Python ML Modeling**  
  Build ML models with Python syntax—without needing any data science skills.

- **Automated Data Preparation**  
  Eliminate tedious data prep with smart automation. Newton integrates with diverse data sources and performs feature engineering automatically, even on messy data.

- **One-Click Deployment**  
  Deploy models quickly and securely with just a click—no complex infrastructure required.

- **AI Assistant Support**  
  A built-in AI Assistant guides users through the full predictive modeling process, from use case identification to deployment.

- **Enterprise-Grade Security**  
  Full data protection and privacy built into the platform.

- **Customer Success Team**  
  AI specialists are available to support users in shaping strategy, testing ideas quickly, and scaling what works.
